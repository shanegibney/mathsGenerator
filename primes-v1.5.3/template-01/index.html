The Current Situation

There are approximately 2,500 refugees in Chios, a small Greek island close to Turkey. Since the political deal on 10th March refugees have been unable to leave the island and are stuck living in desperate conditions. They are unable to work to support themselves but include teachers, translators and others who volunteer to help the most vulnerable.

  • Aegean
  • Olympic Air
  • Ryanair
  • Aer Lingus
 TRAVEL BY FERRY (direct Ferries)
  • Piraeus to Chios ferry - 8 hour overnight boat to Chios from Athens, cabins available
 ACCOMMODATION
  • Chios sun rooms Rooms to let, called ‘Sunrooms’ run by Toula and Monica Kitromilidi, are located on the resort of Megas Limionas, 50 meters from the sea. Shared accommodation €5 a night
 CAR HIRE
  • Toula also organises inexpensive car hire at €20 per day
